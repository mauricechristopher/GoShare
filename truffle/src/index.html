Wait, Did You Say Rewards?

Sure Did!

When someone creates a contract, they have two options to reward users. The first is if they are selling/creating a product, the contributers can be rewarded in tokens. These tokens can be exchanged for the product described in the contract. If the contract creator is not creating/selling a product, they can issue contributers unique digital collectibles. These UDC's can be collected or traded on our asset exchange for Shares.
